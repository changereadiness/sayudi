Change Readiness Briefing

A structured assessment to evaluate whether your organization is prepared to successfully execute transformation.

Why readiness matters

Many transformation initiatives fail not because the strategy is wrong, but because organizations underestimate the human and operational conditions required for successful adoption.

The Six Dimensions of Readiness

1. Clarity of Purpose

Ensuring stakeholders understand the reason for change and the desired outcomes.

2. Stakeholder Alignment

Assessing whether key groups are aligned and prepared to support the transformation.

3. Change Culture

Evaluating organizational openness, adaptability, and capacity for change.

4. Executive Ownership

Understanding leadership commitment, sponsorship, and accountability.

5. People Engagement

Assessing communication, involvement, and employee readiness.

6. Delivery Readiness

Evaluating whether processes, resources, and capabilities support execution.

The Outcome

The Change Readiness Briefing provides leaders with a clear understanding of organizational strengths, potential risks, and priority actions before major transformation initiatives begin.
